What is the Internet ?

 By Prince Sharma
12 Aug 2020

The Internet is huge network of different computer network system exist in different region on our earth.Internet connects millions of computers together and forming a network in which computer can communicate with other computer system and can share data but this can happen as long as they are connected. The Internet uses the standard Internet Protocol suite(TCP/IP) for the continuous communication between computers across the world.

Why was the Internet invented ?

 The Internet was created for researcher, educators and government agencies to share their information through their computer.

History of the Internet

 The Internet was created in the year of 1969.It began with the creation of network of computing devices known as ARPNET this project is sponsored by US Department of Defense .In 1985 the NSFnet was created it is series of networks for research and education communication and it act as a backbone to the ARPNET.These two network were linked together and form the Internet.In the beggining it was only use for military and academic purpose but later it was joined by mainy organisation that are mainly private. In 1990s WWW was developed and Internet gained a massive popularity globally.
